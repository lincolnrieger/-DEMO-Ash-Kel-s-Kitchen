Our Story

The Cook & The Chef.

Ashley and Kelly Parr — a great husband and wife team who have combined all their passions into a business with real heart.

About Us

A partnership built on food.

Ashley and Kelly Parr are the real life version of the Cook and the Chef. With a creative flair and a love of experimenting with new and delicious flavours, they look forward to sharing their talent at your next function.

Together they bring something unique — the precision and technique of a professionally trained chef, combined with the warmth and intuition of a natural cook. The result is food that's both impressive and genuinely satisfying.

Catering for school groups, adult retreats, corporate functions, birthdays and weddings — no event is too big or too personal. We use only the freshest ingredients and source local Adelaide Hills produce where possible.

If you don't find a menu that suits your needs or your budget, please don't hesitate to ask us to design one for you.

"Together we are able to combine the artistry of a commercially trained chef with the natural flair of a great home style cook. And we have lots of fun doing it."

Ashley Parr
Chef

Ashley began as an apprentice chef at age 15 and studied at Tafe SA. He has worked in many well-known kitchens — the Three Brothers Arms, The Magic Flute, and the Lenzerheide Restaurant — before becoming Head Chef of Dixon's Restaurant. He brings over a decade of industry experience to every event.

Kelly Parr
Cook

Kelly has 10 years of hospitality experience, including 5 years as assistant restaurant manager of the Goodwood Park Hotel. Auditioned and shortlisted for Masterchef in 2009, she has a strong knowledge of food flavours and an extensive repertoire of dishes from elegant dinner parties to large-scale events.
